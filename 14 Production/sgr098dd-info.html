SGR098DD - Midnight Society

Back 2 Basics has been one of our label's most successful series of EPs. Now, DJ/Producer Curtis Atchison of Midnight Society compiles the official compilation of Back 2 Basics singles along with a couple of brand new grooves to rock your dance floors. From the disco-infused, "Dancin' (All Night Long)", to tracks like "Ibiza Sunrise" and "The Big Beat" showcasing the yin and yang of Tribal House, there's something for every fan of House music in this ten track sampler.
