SGR105DD - Mause

Trazer na bateria tribais!!! SoundGroove Records welcomes back DJ/Producer Mause from Brazil with one of the standout tracks from the label's ADE compilation. "E Morio!" has everything you need to make your dance floors jump - Tribal drums, a heavy basslines, pianos, synths, and a spiritual chant that will make anyone want to sing along. If that wasn't enough, it also comes with amazing remixes from Midnight Society, Roman Rosari, Leo Z and Guz Sanotto. Also included with this package is another killer track entitled "Evolution". And if you remember Mause's debut EP on SoundGroove then you're more than ready for it!
