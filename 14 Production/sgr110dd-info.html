SGR110DD - This is SoundGroove - 9th Anniversary Edition

It's nearly impossible to reduce nine years of amazing electronic dance music into one compilation. Our next release is more of a sampling of what SoundGroove Records is all about. Compiled by DJ/Producer Curtis Atchison of Midnight Society, this special Ninth Anniversary release features twenty of the label's significant musical moments, as well as previously unavailable remix to "Deepest Afrika" from Tribal Degeneration. Whether if you're a veteran or brand new to the SoundGroove Records sound, this compilation is for you.
